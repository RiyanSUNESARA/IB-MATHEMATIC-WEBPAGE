To begin with first you need to get it clear with basic concepts of Trigonometry, involving Sin, Cos and Tan Values as they will be handy in some concepts explored in Complex Numbers, if you are not comfortable with Trigonometry you would waste a lot of time first understanding them and then Solving Them. When you are studying Complex Numbers you have to pay attention at every minute piece of information, for example what a big difference Arg(z) and arg(z) can make, so you bed to be careful. Complex Numbers revolves around a lot of Theorems, hence you have to thoroughly understand them and you able to image them in your mind as soon as you see a Problem about that Theorem.
